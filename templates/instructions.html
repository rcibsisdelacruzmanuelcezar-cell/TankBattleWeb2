Tank Landship is a strategic turn-based battle game where you command a fleet of tanks against an opponent. The objective is to destroy the enemy's command tank while protecting your own.

2. Tank Placement

Deploy your tanks on a 20x20 grid. You must place:

  • 4 Light Tanks (2 squares each)
  • 2 Medium Tanks (3 squares each)
  • 1 Heavy Tank (4 squares)
  • 2 Tank Destroyers (2 squares each)
  • 1 Command Tank (3 squares) - Destroying this wins the game

Use the Rotate button (R key) to switch tank orientation between horizontal and vertical. Click Random Placement to auto-deploy tanks.

3. Battle Phase

Take turns firing at opponent's board:

  • Click a cell on the enemy board to fire
  • Hit: You continue firing until you miss
  • Miss: Turn passes to opponent
  • Track your hits (red) and misses (gray) on the board

üí° Strategy Tips

  • Spacing: Spread your tanks out to avoid losing multiple units to a single hit
  • Protect the Command Tank: This is your weakest point - position it carefully
  • Search Pattern: When fighting AI, use a systematic search pattern to find enemy tanks
  • Chain Attacks: If you hit a target, continue attacking nearby cells to destroy it
  • Opponent Behavior: Watch the AI's targeting pattern to predict its strategy
